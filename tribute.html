Optimus Prime

The leader with "the touch"

Here's the detailed journey of how Optimus Prime came to life:

  • 9 million years BC: Orion Pax - a dock-worker at a storage yard.

    • He was then killed by Megatron, leader of the Decepticons.

    • Alpha Trion rebulit him, thus the great Optimus Prime - leader of the Autobots - was born.

  • 1984: Both sides awoke after 1000 millenials in stasis as an aftermath of crash landing on Earth, began the Great War III.
  • 2005: After being heavily wounded at the battle of Autobot City, Optimus Prime passed away, with the Matrix of Leadership handed to Hot Rod.
  • 2007: In an effort to get rid of the Hate Plague, Optimus Prime is alive once more.
